]]> NFR (National Finals Rodeo) fans will be highly appreciated to learn the top 15 qualify for the NFR 2021 in accordance with the PRCA and WPRA standings which are updated as of October 26, 2021. The Las Vegas NFR will be held at the Thomas & Mack Arena from December 2-11, 2021.

Congratulations to all the cowboys and cowgirls who have finished the season in the top 15 of the PRCA and WPRA’s standings in seven main events: steer wrestling, team roping, saddle bronc riding, tie-down roping, bareback riding, barrel racing, and bull riding.
