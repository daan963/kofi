Tim O’Connell is a common name in the NFR sport, considering that he has won this event previously three times in a row from 2016-2018. He is set to add another championship to his name, making it the fourth trophy; if he wins. He will have to surpass the 2019 world champion Clayton Biglow, who ended his winning streak and sits at 5th place.

At 15th place is Zach Hibler, who is eyeing to qualify in the NFR for the first time, and behind him is Taylor Broussard, who has previously qualified one time and will be looking for his 2nd NFR.

At the moment, the standings are not yet complete with the Golden Buckle Knockout, which features the top bareback and saddle bronc riders in the world is set to determine the final standings by Wednesday, Sept 30
