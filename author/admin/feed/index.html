The crowd of star cricketers is increasing now in the national elections. Former national team captain Mashrafe Bin Mortaza has become a member of parliament for the second time.The current captain Shakib Al Hasan has also become a member of Parliament this time.  

Both of them are still playing. They will also play in the BPL starting tomorrow. Mashrafe Sylhet Strikers and Shakib’s team Rangpur Riders. Tamim Iqbal is a long time teammate of these two. Will you congratulate them for being members of parliament?

In response to such a question, he told reporters after the practice of Fortune Barisal at BKSP on Thursday, “None of the two have met me yet. If we meet, we will definitely talk. Let’s see what happens then. ‘

He said, “This is a very risky thing, brother. I said no, then it happened after ten years. Then you’ll pretend I wasn’t saying that. Never say no to anything. But as of now I have no such plans. ‘

Current BCB President Nazmul Hasan Papon has taken charge as Sports Minister. He also said to leave the responsibility of the board. Shakib said that he wants to go and take this responsibility at some point. Tamim also has such a dream?

He said, ‘You never know where the future will take you. If Allah writes something like that on my forehead, it will happen automatically. I can’t take it by force. ‘

Mashrafe will play in BPL from the first match

It was almost evening. Mashrafe bin Murtaza suddenly came out of the pandal made in the academy ground.The instability of the camera remained to capture him. He tried to run two or three times, but it didn’t seem like he could do much good.  

Mashrafe’s old leg pain increased after the election campaign. Meanwhile Sylhet Strikers announced him as the captain. However, there was still uncertainty whether Mashrafe would play from the first match or not. However, the vice-captain of the team Mohammad Mithun said on Thursday, “As far as we know, he is available. ‘

Mashrafe joined Sylhet’s practice for a while on Thursday. But this time batting-bowling did nothing. Yet the whole team looked motivated. Mohammad Mithun says, Mashrafe is always like this.  

He said, ‘Bhai Mashrafe is not like this now, he was like this before. Blend in with the team very quickly. When he came to the field yesterday, he didn’t practice but he made a match in the 10-15 minutes he was with the team. We all know how Mashrafe Bhai is, no one will have any difficulty in getting along with him. ‘

There was a lot of discussion and criticism before the start of last season’s BPL. The lack of DRS was also talked about a lot. What difference do you see this time with the last season? Such a question was asked to Mithun.  

He said, “As players, we don’t look for so much difference. We are busy preparing how we can do better. We are looking into that. If the difference is, as I said in the team meeting yesterday, DRS is there from the beginning, it is certainly something to be applauded. ‘

The purpose of the tournament is to improve our native cricketers. Of course, domestic players always have a chance. Because it is our only tournament coverage, it is called our biggest stage. The highlights are good if you perform here. That’s why all our cricketers try to talk about the best performance in BPL. ‘
